About Mealow

Mealow is a meal planning solution designed to simplify your daily food preparation. Here you can find, save, and even customize your recipes according to your dietary needs.

Founded in 2024 by a team of food enthusiasts and developers, Mealow aims to help individuals and families eat healthier by making meal planning easy and accessible to everyone.
